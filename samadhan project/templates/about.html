Introduction

The Driver Drowsiness Detection System is an innovative project aimed at enhancing road safety by preventing accidents caused by drowsy driving. It utilizes advanced computer vision techniques to monitor the driver's state and provide timely alerts when signs of drowsiness are detected.

Key Features

  • Real-time monitoring of driver's facial features
  • Detection of drowsiness indicators such as eye closure and head position
  • Audio alerts to warn the driver when drowsiness is detected
  • Customizable settings for sensitivity and alert preferences

Benefits

The Driver Drowsiness Detection System offers numerous benefits including:

  • Reduction in the risk of accidents caused by drowsy driving
  • Enhanced driver safety and well-being
  • Increased awareness and alertness during long drives
  • Integration with existing vehicle safety systems
